### Velociraptor
**Category:** Threat Hunting  
**Description:**  
Velociraptor is an endpoint visibility and collection tool designed for threat hunting and DFIR.

**Usage:**  
- Collect forensic artifacts at scale  
- Run hunts across thousands of endpoints  
- Perform live response and investigations

**Strengths:**  
- Extremely powerful and flexible  
- Open-source

**Notes:**  
Requires careful access control due to its power.
