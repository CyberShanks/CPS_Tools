### Kibana (Threat Hunting)
**Category:** Threat Hunting  
**Description:**  
Kibana enables exploratory analysis and hypothesis-driven threat hunting over indexed telemetry.

**Usage:**  
- Search logs for suspicious patterns  
- Visualize attacker behavior  
- Build custom hunting dashboards

**Strengths:**  
- Tight integration with Elastic SIEM  
- Excellent visualization capabilities
