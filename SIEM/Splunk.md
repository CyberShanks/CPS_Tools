### Splunk
**Category:** SIEM  
**Description:**  
Splunk is a powerful log aggregation and analytics platform used for security monitoring, threat detection, and incident investigation.

**Usage:**  
- Centralize logs from endpoints, servers, network devices, and cloud services  
- Build correlation searches and alerts  
- Perform threat hunting and forensic analysis using SPL

**Strengths:**  
- Extremely flexible search language  
- Massive ecosystem and integrations  

**Notes:**  
Commonly used in enterprise SOCs; licensing cost can be significant.
